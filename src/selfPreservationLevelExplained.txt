Agression
	= 0: Flee if predator is seen. Attack non predators in diet
	= 1: Flee if larger predator is seen. Attack smaller non predators or predators in diet.
	= 2: If energy of this animal is less than 50, use level 3. Use level 1 otherwise. If isCannibalistic, attack same species as well.
	= 3: Attack everything. If isCannibalistic, attack same species as well.
